Statement
Hello! My name is Evan Pompa (He/Him). I am a 20 year old aspiring game developer who wants to create fun unique experiences through video games.

Growing up, video games gave me a sense of wonder, a sense of joy. My dream is to extend these emotions to future generations. To make the people of tommorow happy.
Game Projects
Bean Reaper
Bean Reaper is a 2D platformer game where you can teleport to thrown projectiles. This game was made for a game jam in Unity, all work was done by me except for music and sfx. Click here to check it out!
You're Fired!
'You're Fired' is a 3rd person action game where you play as a bullet trying to eliminate targets. This game was also made in Unity for a game jam, and I worked as a game designer. Click here to check it out!
TimeLine
TimeLine is a 2D platformer where you can use your past actions to interact with the present. All work except for the sound effects were done by me. Click here to check it out!
