You're Fired
About The Game

What is the game?
"You're Fired" is a 3D Traversal game where you play as a bullet eliminating targets. The player can slow down time with right click or space, and must defeat the final enemy to beat the stage.
My role on the development team?
I was a level designer/creator and a style designer for this project.
Why did you make this project?
I made this project for the 2023 "Wave Jam" hosted on Itch.io by "Zayn". Play The Game Here
