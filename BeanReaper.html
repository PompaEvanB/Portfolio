Bean Reaper
About The Game

What is the game?
Bean Reaper is a 2D platformer when the player can teleport to projectiles. Additionally, The player cannot jump, and must use the projectiles to cross gaps and ascend inclines.
Who was on the development team?
I was the sole programmer, designer, and artist. An additional member made the music, and the sound effects are royaltyfree.
Why did you make this project?
I made this project for the 2023 "Jame Gam #29" hosted on Itch.io by "FranklyFied" and "tiimo". Play The Game Here
